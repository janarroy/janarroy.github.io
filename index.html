Projects & Artifacts

CRM Automation — Email Outreach Platform

I built a CRM tool that connected Google Sheets and Gmail to fully automate email outreach. Before, campaigns took hours of manual effort. With the tool, we reached 15,000+ contacts, improved response rates by 15%, and cut send time by 3× across three committees. It was simple enough that the group kept using it after I handed it off, showing it was scalable and sustainable.

Quokka Brew — Can Design Research & Iterations

Page 1/1

Quokka Brew is a student-founded coffee brand that needed a stronger shelf presence. I conducted 100+ customer interviews and shelf tests, finding that only about 29% of people could identify flavors from a distance. I helped design 20+ prototypes with bigger logos, clear nutrition bands, and bold flavor colorways. Recognition rates jumped by 40%+ and the founders walked away with a scalable design system they could apply across SKUs.

Clear Clinical — Product Strategy & UX

Page 1/1

Clear Clinical is a skincare startup that wanted to scale digital sales. I led user interviews and surveys where about 30% of respondents said credibility cues like before-and-after photos were most important. From that, I built a roadmap with short-term wins like trust-building content and a cleaner site flow, plus long-term moves like influencer partnerships and brand consistency. The plan gave the team a launch strategy backed by real data and early testing suggested engagement could grow by 30%+.
