CRM Automation — Email Outreach Platform

I built a CRM that connects Google Sheets and Gmail with Python so outreach runs on its own. It pulls fresh rows from Sheets, fills a template with merge fields, queues sends to stay under Gmail limits, retries failures, and logs back to the sheet. With this live we hit 15,000+ contacts, responses lifted about 15%, and total send time dropped to roughly one-third across three committees. New campaign = new sheet + a template. Simple, fast, reusable.

Quokka Brew — Can Design Research & Iterations

We ran 60+ interviews then pressure-tested concepts with the 10-ft / 5-ft / 1-ft rule. Takeaways: calmer neutrals read “coffee,” the logo and health callouts need to hit at 5 ft, and flavor cues should be super clear at 1 ft. We benchmarked against Rise, La Colombe, and Starbucks and turned the signal into 3 MVP layouts the team could keep iterating. My job was pulling the insights and pushing changes that actually show up on the can — bigger mark, clean info bands, fast-scan flavor hierarchy.
