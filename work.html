CRM Automation — Email Outreach Platform

During my time at BTG I built a CRM tool that connected Google Sheets and Gmail through Python. The system automatically pulled contact data, generated personalized emails, and sent them out at scale. It ended up reaching over 15,000 contacts, boosted response rates by 15%, and sped up outreach by more than 300% across three committees. What made it valuable was that it wasn’t just a one-off script — I designed it so the process could be reused for future campaigns. Working with the Google Sheets API, Gmail API, and handling JSON data gave me real experience building something technical that also had a direct business impact.
